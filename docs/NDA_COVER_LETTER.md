## 1. Purpose of Disclosure

BhojanOS is a proprietary, multi-tenant Restaurant Operating System built in-house to enable independent food businesses — cloud kitchens, restaurants, and QSR operators — to launch **branded direct-ordering storefronts**, manage kitchen operations, accept payments, and grow repeat customers **without aggregator commission**.

We are providing access to technical, operational, and corporate documentation so that the Receiving Party may evaluate:

- Authenticity and ownership of intellectual property  
- Platform architecture, scalability, and operational resilience  
- Revenue model integrity and commercial traction  
- Legal, trademark, and data-protection posture  
- Team continuity and transition readiness  

All information disclosed pursuant to this process is **Confidential Information** as defined in the NDA and may be used solely for evaluation of a potential transaction.

## 2. Representations Regarding Intellectual Property

BhojanOS represents that, to the best of management’s knowledge:

1. The platform was **developed from first principles** by the founding engineering team and was **not** purchased, white-labelled, or substantially derived from third-party marketplace scripts (including CodeCanyon, ThemeForest, or public “Bhojon/Bhojan POS” boilerplates).

2. All proprietary source code, product designs, trade secrets, and documentation disclosed in the VDR are owned by or exclusively licensed to **[Insert Company Legal Name]**, subject to standard open-source library dependencies under permissive licenses (MIT, Apache 2.0, and similar).

3. The Company will provide, or has provided in the VDR, **Intellectual Property Assignment Agreements** for founders, employees, contractors, and agency partners who contributed to the codebase.

4. The platform does **not** rely on unauthorized web scraping or unofficial integrations with third-party food-delivery aggregators. BhojanOS is architected as the **owned direct channel**; it does not ingest Swiggy or Zomato order feeds.

These representations are made in good faith and are subject to verification through VDR review, technical audit, and customary transaction counsel.
